# erl Cheatsheet

to quit the promt or manage jobs enter `C-g` then `q` or `?` to show the following:
> --> ?  
> c [nn]            - connect to job  
> i [nn]            - interrupt job  
> k [nn]            - kill job  
> j                 - list all jobs  
> s [shell]         - start local shell  
> r [node [shell]]  - start remote shell  
> q                 - quit erlang  
> ? | h             - this message
